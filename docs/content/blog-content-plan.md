## 2. Editorial flag key

Every post in §3–§8 may carry one or more of these flags in the Notes column. Resolve the flag before drafting begins.

| Flag | Meaning | Required action before drafting |
|------|---------|----------------------------------|
| 🔧 **TOOL-NAME** | Post requires naming specific third-party tools | Reframe as an evaluation guide ("what to look for") rather than a ranked list, OR limit to publicly verifiable tools and do not imply partnership. See do-not-invent-rules.md §15 |
| 💰 **PRICING** | Post covers Sirius Systems or market pricing | Market-rate context is allowed. Sirius Systems pricing must not be stated (unconfirmed). Add disclaimer: "Pricing varies — book a discovery call for a quote specific to your setup." |
| ⚖️ **LEGAL** | Post touches compliance, law, or regulatory territory | Add awareness disclaimer. Do not present as legal advice. Flag for founder review before publish. |
| 🔁 **OVERLAP** | Closely overlaps another post in the backlog | Confirm differentiation before drafting. Merge or cut one if they would compete for the same query. |
| 📋 **COMPARISON** | Names or implies a comparison to competitors or alternative categories | No invented claims about named competitors. Frame comparison as category analysis, not vendor evaluation. |

## 9. Topic ideation guardrails (unchanged)

1. Specific buyer question — not a category overview posing as a post.
