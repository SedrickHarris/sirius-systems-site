## 7. Industry-specific blog series (parallel to clusters)

When an industry page is gaining traffic but its mapped service pages aren't converting, fill the gap with industry-targeted posts:

- "Lead capture for home services after hours"
- "Booking automation for cleaning companies with recurring contracts"
- "Why junk removal is a same-day SEO problem"
- "Real estate follow-up sequences that actually get answered"
- "Quote follow-up for contractors: the 48-hour rule"
- "Why coaches and consultants don't need a full website yet (and what to build instead)"

Schedule these reactively, not preemptively.

## 8. Repurposing notes (out of Phase 1)

For each long-form post, we expect later (post-launch):
- A LinkedIn / IG short
- An email newsletter teaser
- Optional 60–90s vertical video walking the core insight

The blog post is the durable, indexable asset. Other formats are downstream.

## 9. Topic ideation guardrails

When proposing new topics, every topic must pass:

1. **Specific buyer question?** "What does AI automation do for me?" — yes. "AI is everywhere now" — no.
2. **Maps to a cluster?** If not, why are we writing it?
3. **Real expertise behind it?** If we can't write it without making things up, we don't write it.
4. **Long enough to be useful, short enough to be read?** See post-type guide in `blog-strategy.md`.
